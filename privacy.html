Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Growth System (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the InnoPulse innovation assessment at innopulse.thegrowthsystem.co.za, in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

Information we collect

When you use the assessment, we may collect:

  • Profile information: your name (optional — you may complete the assessment anonymously), your company or organisation name, and your industry.
  • Contact information: your full name, email address, and phone number, which you provide in order to view your results and be contacted.
  • Assessment information: your responses to the assessment questions and the resulting scores, bands, and insights generated from them.
  • Communication preferences: whether you have opted in to receive email notifications from us.

We do not collect banking details, identity-document numbers, or any special categories of personal information through this assessment.

How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • generate and display your innovation assessment results;
  • contact you to offer a consultation based on your assessment results;
  • send you email notifications only if you have opted in to receive them;
  • improve our assessment and services through aggregated, anonymised analysis.

We rely on your consent as the lawful basis for processing your information for these purposes. You may withdraw your consent at any time (see "Your rights" below).

How your information is stored and shared

Your responses and contact details are stored in Google Sheets (operated by Google) used by The Growth System to manage assessment submissions. Access is limited to authorised members of The Growth System who need it to contact you and deliver the services described above.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except service providers (such as Google) that host or process the data on our behalf, and only as necessary to provide the service.

How long we keep your information

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide consultation follow-up and the services you have requested, and thereafter only as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

Your rights under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • access the personal information we hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate information;
  • object to processing or withdraw your consent;
  • request that we delete your personal information;
  • lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@thegrowthsystem.co.za.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against loss, unauthorised access, and misuse. The assessment is served over a secure (HTTPS) connection.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent version.

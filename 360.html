What an InnoPulse 360 engagement includes

When a company commissions The Growth System, we assess innovation capability across every stakeholder group and aggregate it into one executive-ready diagnosis.

Company-level profile

Responses are collected under a single company campaign and aggregated into an overall innovation capability score and maturity level.

Stakeholder comparison

See how executives, employees, customers, and service providers each experience innovation — and where their perceptions diverge.

Pillar heatmap & gap analysis

A stakeholder-by-pillar heatmap surfaces strengths, blockers, and the biggest gaps between groups.

Executive report & roadmap

Top strengths, top weaknesses, recommended next steps, and a consulting roadmap drawn from the data.

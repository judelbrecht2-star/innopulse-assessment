InnoPulse 360 is a commissioned, multi-stakeholder innovation capability assessment that combines the views of executives, employees, customers and partners into one executive-ready organisational diagnosis — not a public quiz, but a private engagement.

What an InnoPulse 360 engagement includes

When a company commissions The Growth System, we assess innovation capability across every stakeholder group and aggregate it into one clear diagnosis.

Company-level profile

Responses collected under a single company campaign and aggregated into an overall capability score and maturity level.

Stakeholder comparison

See how executives, employees, customers and service providers each experience innovation — and where they diverge.

Pillar heatmap & gap analysis

A stakeholder-by-pillar heatmap surfaces strengths, blockers and the biggest gaps between groups.

Executive report & roadmap

Top strengths, top weaknesses, recommended next steps and a consulting roadmap drawn from the data.

Five capability pillars

Every stakeholder group answers questions mapped to the same five pillars, phrased from their own perspective.

01 · Strategic Innovation Intent02 · Innovation Process03 · People & Capability04 · Culture & Environment05 · Implementation & Value

Built for every stakeholder voice

At minimum, four groups assess the organisation. Additional groups can be added per campaign.

Executives

Strategy, intent and the resourcing of innovation from the top.

Employees

How innovation is really experienced day to day on the ground.

Customers & beneficiaries

The outside-in view of whether innovation creates real value.

Service providers & partners

How the wider ecosystem experiences working with you.

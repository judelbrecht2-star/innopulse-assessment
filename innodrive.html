InnoDrive Programme

InnoDrive: Turning Capability Into Momentum

A hands-on programme that combines assessment, coaching and delivery support to move your organisation from knowing about innovation to doing it consistently.

Home › Services › InnoDrive

InnoDrive is The Growth System's structured innovation programme that pairs capability building with real delivery — so your teams improve how they innovate while making tangible progress on live challenges. Rather than a one-off course, InnoDrive is designed as a sustained drive that builds the habits, structures and momentum innovation needs to take hold.

What InnoDrive combines

Baseline & focus

An InnoPulse assessment to pinpoint the capability gaps that matter most.

Skills & methods

Practical training so teams share a common innovation language and toolkit.

Coaching

Ongoing guidance as teams apply what they learn to real challenges.

Delivery support

Help moving selected ideas through the system to demonstrable outcomes.

Leadership alignment

Engaging sponsors so the work is backed, funded and protected.

Embedding

Installing the structures that keep momentum after the programme ends.

Why a programme, not a workshop

Capability is a habit, and habits form through repetition with support — not a single intense session. InnoDrive is built around that reality: it sustains attention long enough for new behaviours to take root, ties learning to real outcomes so leaders see value, and leaves behind structures rather than just enthusiasm. The result is momentum that continues under its own power.

Start with a baseline. Every InnoDrive begins with an honest measure of where your innovation capability stands today.

Frequently asked questions

What is InnoDrive?

InnoDrive is The Growth System's structured innovation programme that combines capability assessment, training, coaching and delivery support so teams improve how they innovate while making real progress on live challenges.

How is InnoDrive different from a training course?

A course transfers knowledge; InnoDrive builds habits and outcomes over time, pairing learning with coaching and real delivery so the capability sticks and produces value.

Does InnoDrive include the InnoPulse assessment?

Yes. The programme begins with an InnoPulse capability baseline so effort is focused on the gaps that will unlock the most value.

Can InnoDrive be tailored to our organisation?

Yes. The programme is shaped around your strategy, sector and the live challenges your teams are working on. Speak to us about format and scope.

Frequently asked questions

Where should we start?

Most organisations start by measuring their baseline with the free InnoPulse assessment, then use the results to decide whether strategy, systems, capability or delivery support is the priority.

Do we have to take all the services?

No. The services are modular. You can take a single workshop, a strategy engagement, or a full outsourced arrangement &mdash; whatever matches your need and maturity.

Do you work across South Africa?

Yes. We serve organisations across South Africa, including Gauteng, Johannesburg and Pretoria, in person and online.

How do these services relate to ISO 56001?

Together they build the innovation management system that ISO 56001 certifies. You can pursue certification readiness directly through our ISO 56001 advisory service.

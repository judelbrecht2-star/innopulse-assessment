FAQ

Innovation questions, answered

What is an innovation capability assessment?

An innovation capability assessment measures how well an organisation can repeatedly generate, develop and scale new value. InnoPulse scores five pillars and returns a maturity profile showing strengths, gaps and the highest-leverage priorities.

What is innovation management?

Innovation management is the deliberate practice of organising strategy, processes, people and measurement so an organisation can innovate reliably rather than by chance — the discipline an innovation management system and ISO 56001 put into practice.

What is ISO 56001?

ISO 56001 is the international standard, published in 2024, that specifies the requirements for an innovation management system. It is the first standard in its family that an organisation can be independently certified against.

Why do innovation efforts fail?

Most innovation efforts fail not from a shortage of ideas but from structural gaps: strategy that is never funded, no clear process or decision gates, a culture that punishes intelligent failure, or missing measurement. Building capability means treating these as one connected system.

How does The Growth System help companies innovate?

We help South African organisations measure their innovation capability with InnoPulse, then build it through innovation strategy, management systems, training, culture work and ISO 56001 readiness — including hands-on and outsourced delivery.

The five vital signs

Innovation works as a system.

A weakness in one pillar can cap the rest. InnoPulse shows you where energy is flowing — and where it’s getting stuck.

01 / 05

Strategic
intent

A clear, funded innovation ambition linked to business strategy.

What we examine

Whether leaders have translated ambition into deliberate choices, funded priorities and a portfolio connected to measurable business value.

  • Strategic alignment
  • Leadership commitment
  • Portfolio focus
02 / 05

Innovation
process

The gates and portfolio governance that move ideas to launch.

What we examine

How ideas enter the system, become experiments and earn further investment through clear evidence, decisions and accountable governance.

  • Idea intake
  • Experimentation
  • Decision gates
03 / 05

People &
capability

Leadership, skills and the safety to experiment and learn.

What we examine

Whether people have the skills, time, ownership and leadership support to turn uncertainty into learning without fear or organisational friction.

  • Innovation skills
  • Clear ownership
  • Psychological safety
04 / 05

Culture &
environment

The behaviours that make experimentation safe and routine.

What we examine

How incentives, collaboration and everyday leadership behaviour encourage curiosity, constructive challenge and learning from evidence.

  • Collaboration
  • Learning behaviour
  • Aligned incentives
05 / 05

Implementation
& value

Measurement and the discipline to scale what works.

What we examine

Whether validated ideas move into operations with accountable owners, adoption support and benefits that remain visible after launch.

  • Operational handover
  • Value tracking
  • Repeatable scaling

Good questions

Before you take the pulse.

The essentials, explained without the consulting fog.

What is an innovation capability assessment?

It is a structured way to measure how well your organisation turns ideas into value. InnoPulse looks beyond idea volume to the strategy, processes, people, culture and delivery systems that make innovation repeatable.

Who should take the free Pulse Check?

Anyone who wants an honest read on how innovation is experienced in their organisation. It is useful for leaders, managers, innovation teams and employees alike.

What makes InnoPulse Full-Scale different?

It compares perspectives across stakeholder groups, revealing gaps that a single survey average can hide. The result is an executive-ready diagnosis and a clearer roadmap for action.

What happens after the assessment?

You receive a clear view of strengths and blockers. The Growth System can then help you prioritise improvements through strategy, ISO 56001 advisory, training, idea management and hands-on implementation support.
